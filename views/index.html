Spiritual DNA

Why are we called Elijah Centre?

Elijah Centre (EC) has a God-ordained mandate from the Lord to:

  • Exalt the Name, Person, Character and Word of the Lord Jesus Christ in the midst of our order.
  • Bring reformation and restructuring to the global Church;
  • Proclaim to all the nations of the earth a bold new kingdom advance for the 21st Century.
Who was Elijah?
Elijah was bold and lived by tremendous faith.
He proclaimed the word of the Lord
He challenged the opposing false spirit of Baal.
He rebuilt the broken down altars.
He had a heart for others that they will come into truth.
We too as Elijah Centre live by all of the above.
